Electronics workshop

In our electronics workshop, trained employees - including some master technicians - take care of the repair and service of electronic equipment.

Loudspeaker workshop

In 2001 Music Shop was the first retailer of musical equipment in Turkey to open its own workshop for cabinet manufacturing,reconing, and repairing loudspeakers. We have a huge stock of parts from all the renowned manufacturers, which allows our specialists to overhaul almost any PA system within one week.

Guitar workshop

Our guitar workshop has skilled luthiers and specialists that will ensure excellent maintenance and care of your instrument. We also offer detailed repairs and rebuilds to your instrument according to your wishes and needs. A special highlight of our services is our PLEK machine. Fret polishing, fretboard dressing, neck measurement, re-fretting, and all things related to the frets and fretboard – a PLEK setup will make your guitar as playable as possible.

Piano workshop

Several trained piano builders work in our Piano Department to ensure great after-sales service and care for your instrument.

Strings workshop

String instruments are serviced and repaired in our in-house workshop. Each instrument is checked and adjusted prior to shipping to suit your playing style. It’s also where we check and adjust our own Music Shop brand instruments before shipping.

Wind instruments workshop

Our brass and woodwind workshop takes care of the service and repair of every kind of brass and reed instrument. Even the most exquisite instruments are in good hands with our three brass and woodwind instrument builders.
